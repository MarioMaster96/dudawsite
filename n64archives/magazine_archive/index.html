        Magazine Archive


        I've set up this page to digitally preserve all of the magazines in my collection which feature N64 content. The magazines below are in CBR format, which is basically just a collection of images compiled into a RAR archive. You can use CDisplay or ComicRack to view CBR files, or if you don't like using these programs then you can just extract the pages using WinRAR.

        IMPORTANT: Copyright of all products on this page belongs to the respective owners. I don't claim ownership of any of the content in the Magazine Archive. If you are the owner/publisher of anything on this page and wish to have it removed, please contact me here and I will take it down as specified. Thank you.
